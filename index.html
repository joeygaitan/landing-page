M PAD

Music Production Agile Development

What is it?

M Pad is a music application that allows users to rate songs and watch rate certain parts of a song. This allows the artist to have better songs released for the public. This application will allow users to be able to vote on parts of songs that are bad. A user can make a suggestion, and people can then upvote or down vote the request. If you get enough upvotes votes then you get a verified voter tag for your user.

Who is building it?

We are a team of dedicated engineers who were once in the music industry. We wish we had a software where you could rate our music on the fly rather than have it come out and flop like most of us experience.
